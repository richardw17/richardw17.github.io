Coffee Grind Size Analysis on Your Smartphone


Analyze coffee particle size with your smartphone camera. - Coffee Grind is an app that measures the particle size distribution of your coffee grounds.

When you grind coffee, your grinder produces millions of particles of varying sizes. These particle sizes greatly influence the extraction rate and the total amount of coffee extracted. A more uniform grind leads to more consistent extraction across all particles—resulting in better coffee!
