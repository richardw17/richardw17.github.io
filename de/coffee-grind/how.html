# Ground Coffee Analysis - How does it work?


  • Prepare your coffee grounds spread on a white sheet of paper
  • Place a coin next to it (Coffee Grind scales the particles with its help)
  • Drag on +, Coffee Grind does the rest for you

  • You get a preview of your digital coffee ground spread
  • And your Coffee Grind Size Distribution
