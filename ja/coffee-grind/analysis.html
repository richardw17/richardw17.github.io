# Compare Your Grinding Results



Grind size distribution, often represented as a histogram, illustrates the relationship between particle size and volume or area. Using this data, Coffee Grind allows you to compare different brews, grinders, or beans. It also helps determine whether your grinder burrs are still sharp or need maintenance.
